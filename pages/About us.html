Our History

Our center was founded in 2023 by a professional nurse named RAMALATA AWELANI COLLEN. She works alongside her daughter, RAMALATA THILIVHALI MARRYYJANE, who assists in running the center. RAMACOL is a dedicated reproductive health center located in Limpopo, Thohoyandou.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide quality, accessible, and respectful healthcare for women of all ages. We believe in empowering women through education, access to resources, and personalized medical support.
